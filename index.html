First Post - Introduction

September 24, 2022

Hello World!

Like many blogs out there, I haven't been keeping up with mine. My hope is that I can change that with a series of new blogs posts (but we'll see lol) around things that interest me or have to do with my career.

No, it isn't a formatting issue; this site is intentionally designed with no Javascript and to be HTML5 compliant. This is all 100%, hand-crafted HTML. No templating or generation used here. I wanted to get back to the old school development roots of my childhood and really focus on the simplicity of the "old web." So you won't find any fancy CSS or Javascript, no framework of the week here. Will it create more work for myself? Yeah, probably. But I will have ultimate control over how everything is formatted and I'll get to enjoy that handcrafted feeling. Plus, no silly tracking to worry about and this site will display everywhere that can render HTML5. Finally, this site uses Cloudflare Pages hosting. Another advantage due to the simplicity in development here is the fact I can host this as a static website.

Site back and cruise around; hopefully this doesn't die like every other personal site I've tried before ;)
